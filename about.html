My Background

Hi there! I’m a 23 year old front-end developer who loves turning creative ideas into beautiful, functional websites. There’s something so exciting about starting with a blank page and watching it come to life through design and coding.

I really enjoy working with others—whether it’s brainstorming ideas, solving problems together, or just helping out a teammate. That said, I’m just as happy diving into solo projects where I can take charge and put my own stamp on things. Either way, I always put my best into everything I do and make unique websites each time.

At the heart of it all, I’m just someone who loves what they do and is always excited to learn and grow. Whether it’s tackling a new challenge or finding new ways to make a website shine, I’m always up for it!

My Skills & Expertise

During my studies, I built a strong foundation in front-end development, focusing on creating user interfaces that seamlessly merge customer experience with system development. I learned to develop products that not only look visually appealing but also deliver exceptional functionality and technical precision. My education emphasized a comprehensive approach, combining design processes, interaction design, and programming to prepare me for real-world challenges.

Throughout my time at school, I mastered key programming languages and frameworks, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind, Vite, and Bash. I also gained proficiency with essential tools for front-end development, such as Git, Cypress, Husky, and Github, to manage workflows and collaborate effectively. In addition to coding, I learned to work with industry-standard programs like Figma for design, Netlify for deployment, and Visual Studio Code for code editing and debugging.

My education was hands-on, with practical tasks that mirrored the challenges of the industry. These projects taught me how to optimize workflows, automate tasks, and streamline work processes to deliver high-quality results efficiently. I also explored the principles of entrepreneurship, gaining insight into the business side of development.

I would say the highlights of my education was the projects at the end of each semester. With these I had to be creative with the design and use everything I previously learned to my advantage.

Overall, my studies provided me with the theoretical knowledge and practical expertise needed to excel in a career as a front-end developer.
