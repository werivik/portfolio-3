    My Skills & Expertise

    During my studies, I built a strong foundation in front-end development, focusing on creating user interfaces that seamlessly merge customer experience with system development. I learned to develop products that not only look visually appealing but also deliver exceptional functionality and technical precision. My education emphasized a comprehensive approach, combining design processes, interaction design, and programming to prepare me for real-world challenges.

    Throughout my time at school, I mastered key programming languages and frameworks, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind, Vite, and Bash. I also explored the principles of entrepreneurship, gaining insight into the business side of development.

    I would say the highlights of my education was the projects at the end of each semester. With these I had to be creative with the design and use everything I previously learned to my advantage.
